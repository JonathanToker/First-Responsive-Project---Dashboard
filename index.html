Plans & Future Projects

Follow The Roadmap

Keeping up the learning with following the paved roadmap. This roadmap is a route for learning front-end.

Break Problems Into Smaller Ones

It is very important to break down everything into small pieces so you won't be overwelmed by the complexity or waste time, in addition to that breaking your learnings into smaller pieces allows you to be more knowledable on more specific areas - better than being "jack of all trades".

Going further with FullStack

You always need to keep learning as a human being, especially if you want to deal with technology where innovation is one of the most important things in the field. So as front-end becomes easy, you should keep improving and at the same time learn new stuff on how to deal with the back-end.

Building Projects

The most important thing in both front-end and back-end development is to build your experience and the best way to do so is to build different kind of projects all the time. That's how your mind will compound itself to the new knowledge much better and eventually with time you'll be proficient with web development.
